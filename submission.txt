Story number and name: 1 - Extend User Profile Detail With Usage
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action:

- On each users page there are two new cards:
  1) The most recently uploaded photo. This updates immediately if an image is uploaded while on this page.
  2) The most commented on photo for that user.
- If the user does not have any images, a card shows explaining that the user has no images.

Story number and name: 6 - Photo Like Votes
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action:

- Go to the photos page for any user.
- Every photo will have an empty thumbs up below it on the card.
- Clicking that thumbs up likes the photo.
  - An alert will pop up in the upper right corner alerting you the like was successful.
  - The number of likes next to the like button will increase by 1.
  - The like button will become filled in.
- Clicking the filled in thumbs up button dislikes the photosOfUser.
  - An alert will pop up in the upper right corner alerting you the dislike was successful.
  - The number of likes next to the like button will decrease by 1.
  - The like button will become empty again  .
- Each user may only like any photo one timestamp.
- Multiple users can like the same photo.

Story number and name: 7 - Favorite List of Photos for Users
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action:

- Go to the photos page for any user.
- Every photo will have an empty heart below it on the card.
- Clicking that heart favorites the photo.
  - An alert will pop up in the upper right corner alerting you the favorite was successful
  - The heart will become filled in.
- Clicking the filled in heart unfavorites the photo.
  - An alert will pop up in the upper right corner alerting you the unfavorite was successful
  - The heart will become empty again.
- When a user is logged in, a button appears at the top of the sidebar that takes
  that user to the favorites page.
- The favorites page contains thumbnails of all photos that are favorited by
  the logged in user.
- Underneath each thumbnail is a button that will unfavorite the picture when clicked.
  - An alert will pop up in the upper right corner alerting you the unfavorite was successful.
  - The image will disappear from the favorites page.
- For any image that appears on the favorites page, clicking on the image will open
  a modal containing the image with the time of upload underneath.
